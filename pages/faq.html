We are 100% digital and for your convenience, we give you the option to schedule your delivery on the day and in the time slot that suits you best, you can receive it the same day! On the other hand, if you buy more than 20 beds, you will be assigned a date and time to pick it up directly at our warehouse.

Of course, we have the option of same day delivery or you can schedule it for any other day within one of the four time slots that best suits your needs.

We have 3 routes during the day (morning, afternoon and evening). You can choose in which of the four slots you want your order to arrive:

Band 1: 9:00 am -13:00 pm

Band 2: 11:00 am- 15:00 pm

Band 3: 15:00 pm - 20:00 pm

Band 4: 17:00 pm- 21:00 pm

Or, you can schedule it for another day in the same slots.
